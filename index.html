Four years for Forty Acres

Five months ago, I promised to the Class of 2018 to commence a four year project to transform the Forty Acres. The goal: reform financial aid policy, reconnect students (especially freshman) with Student Government, and reinforcing UT's status as America's "Public Ivy League."

In five months, I became familiar with the power and limits of Student Government. I investigated Student Governments across the country for ideas on how to better connect with and serve students. In the process, I created a campus-wide network of freshman who share my passion for transforming our campus.

To the Class of 2015, 2016, and 2017, I ask you to partner with me. Let's transform these Forty Acres.
